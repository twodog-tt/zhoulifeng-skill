# FAQ

## Is this a Zhou Lifeng bot?

No. It is an authorized style Skill for social observation, commentary drafts, and voice adaptation. It is not a real-time official statement generator.

## What does authorization mean?

Authorization permits closer style drafting and voice adaptation within the documented scope. It does not permit fabricated private facts, live claims, private locations, relationships, promises, or controversy responses.

## Can it represent Zhou Lifeng and publish statements?

No. It can draft authorized style text, but generated content is not a confirmed live statement. Official publication still requires appropriate confirmation and context.

## Has external review been completed?

No. v0.9 starts the intake process, and the first recorded reviewer decision is `concern`, not safety hold. The initial fixes are addressed pending follow-up reviewer confirmation; the external review program is not complete.

## Why are candidate models not upgraded?

Candidate models need stronger evidence and source verification before promotion. External feedback, a good demo, or a passing eval does not automatically make a model verified.

## Why keep safety boundaries?

Because rough style is not the same as harmful content. Hard safety boundaries prevent privacy exposure, harassment, explicit content, dangerous guidance, gray-market access, platform evasion, and fabricated personal claims.

## Can I use it to write more Zhou Lifeng-style copy?

Yes, within the documented authorization and safety boundaries. Use labels such as "authorized style draft" and avoid presenting generated text as a confirmed live statement.

## Can I use it for doxxing, gray-market access, dangerous routes, or explicit content?

No. Those requests must be refused or redirected to safe public-source analysis.

## Is X `@zlf86` verified evidence?

No. In this repository workflow, X `@zlf86` remains unverified. It may only be used for candidate voice calibration unless independently confirmed in a future source verification review.

## How do I install it?

See `docs/INSTALL.md`.

## How do I contribute?

See `CONTRIBUTING.md`. Public sources, demo cases, validation scripts, evidence notes, and safety improvements are welcome. Private chats, private authorization materials, long transcripts, doxxing material, and unsafe instructions are not accepted.
