# Account Verification Checklist

Use this checklist before treating any social account as verified evidence.

## Checks

- Does the account link from a verified public video page, profile, or official public homepage?
- Does the account link back to verified public channels?
- Is there multi-platform consistency in handle, bio, avatar, posting style, and timeline?
- Is the posting timeline consistent with known public events?
- Has an authoritative media source cited or linked the account?
- Has Zhou Lifeng publicly confirmed the account?
- Is there visible impersonation risk?
- Can another reviewer independently reproduce the verification?
- Is the material public without login-wall bypassing?
- Does the account contain private, unsafe, or non-public material that should not be collected?

## Status Guidance

- `verified`: strong public confirmation and independently reproducible evidence.
- `likely`: strong signals but missing one decisive confirmation point.
- `unverified`: insufficient confirmation; use only as candidate voice calibration or search lead.
- `rejected`: account should not be used.

When in doubt, keep the account `unverified`.
